About Company

The most opportune moment to bring your innovative idea to fruition, whether you're a street vendor or an engineer, contemplating a startup, is the "Phela Paisa" brought to initiate the startup. "Phela Paisa" aims to provide an enticing platform for talented youth, startup enthusiasts, and entrepreneurs across the country to harness their out-of-the-box thinking and introduce innovations. You are required to share your idea with us, which will be thoroughly analyzed by top-notch industry experts. If your business idea is selected as the 1st, 2nd, or 3rd choice, you will receive monetary rewards from "First Money".
